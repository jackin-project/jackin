# Research

This section holds research dossiers: external landscape scans, feasibility notes, source ledgers, and brainstorm material. Research can inform roadmap items, but it is not itself an implementation commitment.

Roadmap pages should stay task-shaped: current status, intended implementation, acceptance criteria, and links to the research that justifies the plan. When a research page becomes actionable, promote the resulting task to [/roadmap/](/roadmap/) and keep the evidence here.
