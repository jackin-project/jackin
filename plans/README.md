## How to read this index

- **Priority**: P1 (correctness/security/high-leverage), P2 (real debt/DX/perf), P3 (low-confidence,
  cosmetic, or large speculative spikes). Priority is *advice*, not urgency — the operator asked for
  the full ledger, so P3 ≠ "skip", it means "lower leverage / needs a decision first".
- **Conf** = confidence the finding is real and worth acting on: HIGH (code read, certain),
  MED (strong signal), LOW (smell — plan is *investigate*, not *fix*).
- Investigate/spike plans (LOW conf or L effort) tell the executor to **produce a decision + follow-up
  plan**, not to blindly implement.
